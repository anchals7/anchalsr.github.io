Topic

My website is going to be about animation films, tv shows, and books. These are things that I enjoy a lot, and simply want to put them together in one place. I have explored a lot of these things in life, and they are like my comfort place.

Audience

The audience of this website is anyone interested in seeing some of my favorite animations and books. Whether they are trying to get to know more about me, or simply looking for something they will like.

The pages on the website are titled Home, Books, Animations, About, and Join Us.
